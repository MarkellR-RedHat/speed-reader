Ultra-short summary of a technical paper, document, or web page. Just the key points.

Input: $ARGUMENTS (a file path to a PDF, markdown, or text file, or a URL)

Read the provided document. Then produce exactly 5 to 7 bullet points capturing the most important takeaways. Each bullet should be one sentence. Prioritize findings, results, and implications over background or methodology.

Adapt your focus based on document type:
- **Academic papers:** Lead with the novel result and the headline number. Skip background entirely.
- **RFCs and specs:** Lead with what changes, what breaks, and the timeline.
- **Blog posts:** Lead with any concrete claims or announcements. Flag unsubstantiated hype.
- **Product docs:** Lead with what it does, what it does not do, and integration requirements.

Do not include headers, section labels, or any other formatting beyond the bullet list. This output is meant to be dropped into a Slack message or a quick status update.
